Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Learn how to make soft, chewy and absolutely irresistible chocolate chip cookies. Follow this recipe step by step.

Ingredients

  • Two sticks of butter creamed with white and brown sugars
  • Eggs add moisture and act as a binding agent, which means they help hold the dough together
  • Vanilla extract enhances the overall flavor of the chocolate chip cookies
  • Baking soda acts as a leavener, which means it helps the cookies rise
  • A bit of hot water creates steam as it bakes, working with the baking soda to puff the cookies up
  • A pinch of salt enhances the falovrs of the others ingredients, but it won't make the cookies taste salty
  • All-purpose flour helps create gluten, which add structure to the cookie dough
  • Of course, you'll need semisweet chocolate chips! You can use dark or milk chocolate chips if you prefer
  • Walnuts are optional, but they add nutty flavor and a welcome crunch

Step by step

  1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla
  2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture
  3. Stir in the flour, chocolte chips, and walnuts
  4. Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet
  5. Bake until edges are golden brown

In an oven preheated at 350 degrees F, the chocolate chips cookies should be perfectly baked in about 10 minutes.
